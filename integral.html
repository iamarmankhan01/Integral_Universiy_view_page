Course we offer

Some of the most popular courses offered by Integral University are BTech, MTech, MBA, MBBS and B.Arch.
Admission to all the courses is offered for both National and International Students. .

Intermediate

Admission at Integral University for various UG and PG courses are are open now. MBBS admission at Integral University is based on merit in NEET UG 2023. B.Tech Admission at Integral University is based on JEE score.

Degree

Integral University offers a four-year full-time BTech course at the undergraduate level. Admission to BTech course is completely entrance-based. Integral University offers a regular Bachelor of Technology (BTech) programme across 9 specialisations of Engineering.

Post graduated

The Integrated BTech MTech course, as the name suggests, is a combination of a Bachelors and Masters’ degree in various specialisations of technology or engineering.
